1. Purpose

This agreement serves to strengthen our relationship by expressing our shared values, expectations, and commitment to each other in a respectful, loving, and fun way.

2. Terms of the Relationship

2.1 Commitment:

Both parties agree to be loyal, honest, and supportive of one another.

2.2 Communication:

We promise to communicate openly, calmly, and respectfully — even during disagreements.

2.3 Time Together:

We will spend quality time together regularly.

2.4 Affection Clause: 💌

Hugs, kisses, compliments, and cute texts are encouraged daily.

2.5 Conflict Resolution:

We agree to talk thing through and to always seek peace after a disagreement.

2.6 Trust & Privacy: 🔒

We respect each other's space, privacy, and boundaries. No snooping unless invited.

2.7 Surprise Clause: 🎁

Surprise dates, snacks, or cute notes are highly encouraged.

3. Deal Breakers

Each party agrees to avoid:

  • Dishonesty
  • Disrespectful behavior
  • Ignoring the other's needs or feelings
  • Ghosting or emotional withdrawal

4. Relationship Goals

We both want to:

  • Support each other's dreams
  • Grow individually and as a couple
  • Make each other laugh often 😄
  • Always choose love, even during tough times

5. Duration

This contract is valid from the date signed until further notice (or until either party wishes to revise it in a healthy and respectful way).
